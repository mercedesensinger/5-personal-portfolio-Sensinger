ABOUT ME

I am a student at Andover High interested in dance and art. I have taken dance consistently since I was six, and though I don't take art classes, I often paint and sketch when I have the time. Additionally, I love to read and am always excited to pick up a new and interesting book. I value my schoolwork as well, and do my best to put it as my first priority. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my friends or just relaxing and watching TV.

MY TRAITS

A little more information about me.

1. HARDWORKING: When I'm given a task or assignment to complete, I work to produce the best product possible with my abilities. I always try to make sure I display work that I am proud of.

2. OPTIMISTIC: Even when times get tough, I don't let negativity drag me down, and stay focused on the task at hand. I know that things happen for a reason, and do my best to stay upbeat about things that happen in my life, even if it's difficult.

3. SELF-DISCIPLINED: Though there are many distractions in many forms all around us, I am good about staying focused and working against the temptation to procrastinate on my work. This gives me a better chance of producing my best work.
